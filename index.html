About Our Garage

Ashante Auto Garage (Thika) Ltd is a fully equipped, professionally managed auto repair and body shop serving private motorists, corporate clients, and fleet operators across Thika and its surroundings. We combine hands-on mechanical expertise with modern diagnostic tools to deliver reliable, long-lasting solutions for all vehicle makes and models.

Our team is made up of experienced technicians who are passionate about cars and committed to honest workmanship. We believe in clear communication, fair pricing, and doing the job right the first time. This customer-first approach has earned us a strong reputation and a consistent 4.5★ rating from our clients.

Whether you drive a personal car, a commercial vehicle, or manage a fleet, you can trust Ashante Auto Garage to keep your vehicles safe, efficient, and roadworthy.
